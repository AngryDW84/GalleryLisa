LISA HONG

 

Born in 1984, Seoul, Korea

Raise in Christchurch, New Zealand

2002-2005 University of Canterbury Ilam School of Fine Arts, Bachelor of Fine Arts

____.2008 University of Auckland, Elam School of Fine Arts, Postgraduate Diploma

2014-2015 Massey University, Master of TESOL Leadership
